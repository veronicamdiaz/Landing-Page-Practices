SF7 Lamp

This table lamp has become a coveted designer object and the perfect reflection of Bauhaus design with its basic geometric shapes and asymmetrical elements. It was created in 1927, but its designer is sadly unknown. Its striking hemisphere is made of polished aluminium that partially covers the opal glass. Another spherical element connects the light fixture to the standpipe, which leads to a black painted metal base and the switch.

Akari A1 Lamp

In 1951, Isamu Noguchi began designing his Akari Light Sculptures, works characterized by luminosity and weightlessness. The artist chose the name "akari" for these objects, a Japanese word similar to the English word "light," which evokes the concepts of luminosity and physical lightness. During a trip to Japan, Noguchi visited Gifu, a city known for its production of paper parasols and lanterns. During his stay, he sketched his first two Akari Light Sculptures.

Kaiser Idell Lamp

The Kaiser Idell Table Lamp model 6631-T has a unique and high quality that exudes originality, but also offers exceptional sculptural quality and a rigorously clean design. The collection is centered on the patented swivel joint and the incredibly distinctive shade is easy to recognize, thanks to the stamp on the top that confirms that the design is an "ORIGINAL KAISER-idell".

Nesso Artemide Lamp

Nesso is an icon of Italian design of the 60s, a decade of conquest of modernity in which design redesigns the domestic landscape through the first applications of plastic materials.It was designed by Giancarlo Mattioli.

Bauhaus Medieval Lamp

The Bauhaus Medieval Indoor Table Lamp combines retro charm with modern simplicity, creating a unique piece for any bedside, living room, or workspace. Inspired by iconic Bauhaus design, this glass lamp adds an artful touch while providing a warm, ambient glow.

Poul Henningsenn Lamp

Poul Henningsen emerged from the Scandinavian tradition, initially dedicating himself to traditional architectural projects. Worthy of note is the iconic and refined collection of PH lamps designed by Henningsen for Louis Poulsen.
